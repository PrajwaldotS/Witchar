Robotics Engineering

About Robotics Engineering

Robotics Engineering is an interdisciplinary field that combines mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering to design and build robotic systems. It plays a vital role in automation, manufacturing, space exploration, healthcare, and artificial intelligence.

Future Scope

The future of Robotics Engineering includes advancements in AI-driven robotics, autonomous vehicles, robotic surgery, and industrial automation. Demand for robotics experts is growing in industries such as aerospace, defense, healthcare, and entertainment.

Career Opportunities

  • Robotics Engineer
  • Automation Engineer
  • AI and Machine Learning Specialist
  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Control Systems Engineer
  • Biomechanical Engineer
  • Drone Engineer

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50-60% (varies by institution).
  • Qualify for entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, or other university-specific tests.
