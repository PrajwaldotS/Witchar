Artificial Intelligence Engineering

About Artificial Intelligence Engineering

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Engineering is an interdisciplinary field that focuses on creating smart systems capable of learning, reasoning, and making decisions. It integrates machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and robotics.

Future Scope

The AI industry is rapidly evolving, with applications in healthcare, finance, automation, smart cities, and more. The demand for AI professionals is projected to skyrocket in the coming years, making it one of the most sought-after careers.

Career Opportunities

  • AI Engineer
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • Robotics Engineer
  • Natural Language Processing Expert
  • AI Research Scientist
  • Autonomous Vehicle Engineer

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50-60% (varies by institution).
  • Qualify for entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, or other university-specific tests.
