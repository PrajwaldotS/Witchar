Civil Engineering

About Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a professional discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the built environment, including roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems.

Future Scope

With urbanization and infrastructural development growing rapidly, Civil Engineering remains a crucial field. Opportunities in smart city projects, sustainable construction, and disaster-resistant infrastructure are expanding.

Career Opportunities

  • Structural Engineer
  • Construction Manager
  • Geotechnical Engineer
  • Transportation Engineer
  • Environmental Engineer
  • Urban Planner
  • Water Resource Engineer

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50-60% (varies by institution).
  • Qualify for entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, or other university-specific tests.
