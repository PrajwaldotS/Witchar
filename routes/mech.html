Mechanical Engineering

About Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest and broadest branches of engineering. It deals with the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. This field integrates principles of physics, material science, and mathematics to develop machines and mechanical systems.

Future Scope

The field of Mechanical Engineering is constantly evolving with advancements in robotics, automation, AI-driven manufacturing, and sustainable energy solutions. Emerging fields include nanotechnology, mechatronics, and aerospace engineering.

Career Opportunities

  • Automobile Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer
  • Manufacturing Engineer
  • Thermal Engineer
  • Aerospace Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Research and Development (R&D) Engineer

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50-60% (varies by institution).
  • Qualify for entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, or other university-specific tests.
