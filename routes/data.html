Data Science Engineering

About Data Science Engineering

Data Science Engineering involves using scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to extract insights from structured and unstructured data. It integrates machine learning, big data analytics, and artificial intelligence to make data-driven decisions.

Future Scope

Data Science is a rapidly evolving field with growing demand across industries such as healthcare, finance, e-commerce, and cybersecurity. The future includes advancements in deep learning, AI-driven automation, and quantum computing for data processing.

Career Opportunities

  • Data Scientist
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Big Data Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • AI Research Scientist
  • Business Intelligence Analyst
  • Quantitative Analyst

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50-60% (varies by institution).
  • Qualify for entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, or other university-specific tests.
